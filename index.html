About Me

I'm a Staff Product Designer with roots in Architecture and Business Administration and nearly a decade of experience designing and scaling complex digital platforms. I approach products the way an architect approaches cities—pairing durable systems with intuitive human flow so complexity fades and trust emerges.

Over the past several years, I've led design for $800M+ in revenue-generating products and built design systems that reached 100% adoption across large, cross-functional engineering organizations. At InMarket, I partnered closely with Product, Engineering, and Data Science to translate machine-learning outputs into clear, actionable experiences—helping more than 2,000 brand clients make confident decisions from complex data.

Outside of product design, I'm a published author and photographer. Storytelling is central to how I work: I use narrative to make advanced technology understandable, useful, and human.
